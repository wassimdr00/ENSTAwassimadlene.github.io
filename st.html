Access Conditions

Access to the Foundation Program in Science and Technology at the National Higher School of Advanced Technologies (ENSTA) is reserved for candidates holding the following Baccalaureate streams: Mathematics, Technical Mathematics, and Experimental Sciences, in accordance with the criteria defined in the Baccalaureate Guide for each academic year.
