Higher School of Advanced Technologies

Higher School of Advanced Technologies E.N.S.T.A

Higher School of Advanced technologies are divided into two departments: ST in Beb l'Oued and MI in Dergana. The education system is a five-year engineering program: two preparatory years and three years of specialization. The ST specializations comprise 14 and 2 MI.

This school was created in 2022, following the dissolution of the National Higher School of Technology and the Higher School of Applied Sciences in Algiers.

how to access to our school

Admission to the school is subject to certain conditions, depending on the program. Students who meet these conditions are entitled to admission. A unique feature of the school is that students can join the second or third cycle of postgraduate studies without having previously studied at the same institution, especially if there are specializations offered exclusively at that school
